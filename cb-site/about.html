Our story

In 2019, founded Coderzbot and brought together some brilliant UX UI designers, front-end and back-end developers under one roof. It was a new beginning for them, a quest to find themselves and make their work count.

Aside from their passion, values, and love for food, not mucH has changed since then. Fast forward to today, Coderzbot is a 10+ family that has worked on 100+ projects ranging from tech startups to enterprise design transformation and has touched the lives of over 2 million people. They take pride in the numerous eureka moments along their journey, rather than the numbers.

That's all the time we have to bore you with our story. Despite the fact that everything else about us is exciting!
