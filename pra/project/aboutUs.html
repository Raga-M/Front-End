About Royal Enfield

Welcome to Royal Enfield, a brand that has been synonymous with the romance of riding for over a century.

Royal Enfield is a legendary motorcycle brand that has been in continuous production since 1901. Originally based in Redditch, England, the company has been headquartered in Chennai, India since 1955.

Today, Royal Enfield is known for its iconic motorcycles, including the Classic 350, Himalayan, Interceptor 650, and Continental GT 650. These bikes are renowned for their retro styling, rugged durability, and powerful performance.

At Royal Enfield, we are committed to delivering the ultimate motorcycling experience to our customers. Our mission is to create motorcycles that inspire and empower riders, enabling them to explore new horizons and push their limits.

Over the years, Royal Enfield has built a reputation for producing high-quality, reliable motorcycles with timeless design and unmatched character. The company's motorcycles are known for their distinctive sound and style, and they are highly sought after by motorcycle enthusiasts all over the world.

Royal Enfield's current lineup includes a range of classic and modern motorcycles, including the iconic Bullet, the retro-styled Classic, and the adventure-oriented Himalayan. The company is also known for its custom motorcycle program, which allows customers to customize their bikes with a range of accessories and upgrades.

In addition to its motorcycles, Royal Enfield is also involved in various community-building initiatives, including riding clubs and events, that help bring together enthusiasts from all walks of life. Overall, Royal Enfield is a company that has remained true to its heritage and continues to produce motorcycles that capture the spirit of adventure and exploration. With a global following and a commitment to quality and innovation, Royal Enfield is poised to continue its success for many years to come.
